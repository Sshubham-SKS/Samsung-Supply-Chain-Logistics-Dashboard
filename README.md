## Overview

The Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive business intelligence solution designed to monitor and optimize end-to-end supply chain operations. Built using Power BI, SQL, and Python, the dashboard provides actionable insights into supplier performance, inventory management, shipment tracking, logistics efficiency, and customer revenue trends.

The objective of this project is to transform raw operational data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making, cost optimization, inventory planning, and logistics performance improvement.

---

## Business Problem

Modern supply chains generate large volumes of data across procurement, inventory, transportation, and sales operations. Without centralized analytics, organizations face challenges such as:

* Inventory overstocking or stockouts
* Supplier performance inconsistencies
* Shipment delays and increased logistics costs
* Product quality issues
* Limited visibility into revenue and profitability drivers

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive reporting and KPI-driven analysis.

## Dashboard Modules

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of supply chain performance through key business metrics:

* Gross Revenue
* Total Revenue
* Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Perfect Order %
* Total Shipments

### 2. Supplier Analytics

Evaluates supplier effectiveness and procurement efficiency.

Key Metrics:

* Order Quantity
* Average Lead Time
* Supplier Quality Score
* Unit Cost Analysis
* Supplier Performance Ranking

Business Value:

* Identify top-performing suppliers
* Monitor procurement costs
* Improve sourcing decisions

### 3. Inventory & Production Analytics

Tracks inventory health and production efficiency.

Key Metrics:

* Inventory Value
* Safety Stock
* Inventory Turnover Rate
* Days of Inventory
* Defective Units
* Reorder Point Analysis

Business Value:

* Reduce carrying costs
* Prevent stock shortages
* Improve inventory utilization

### 4. Shipment & Logistics Analytics

Monitors transportation performance and delivery efficiency.

Key Metrics:

* Shipment Cost
* Total Deliveries
* Delivery Status
* Delay Analysis
* Carrier Performance
* Delay Reason Analysis

Business Value:

* Optimize logistics operations
* Improve delivery performance
* Reduce transportation delays

### 5. Customer & Revenue Analytics

Provides insights into customer behavior and revenue generation.

Key Metrics:

* Revenue by Channel
* Profit by Channel
* Product Revenue Contribution
* Discount Analysis
* Revenue Growth Trends

Business Value:

* Understand revenue drivers
* Optimize pricing strategies
* Improve customer profitability

## Key Business Insights

* Inventory turnover indicates effective stock utilization.
* Certain logistics carriers contribute disproportionately to shipment delays.
* Supplier lead-time analysis identifies opportunities for procurement optimization.
